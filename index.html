HPP Overview

Do you have a need to accept payments on your website? Sometimes what seems like a simple task can quickly become overwhelming as you look into the options of accepting payments online. Then pile on the complexity of security, with the number of data breaches over the past few years, small business can’t take the risk of being the next headline for a data breach.

You have a simple need, you want to take payments or donations on your website to help your business be more efficient. Our newest payment button and hosted payment page may be just the thing your company needs to get started to accepting payments online.

How it works

The Clearent payment button allows businesses to add a payment option to their website by simply copy and pasting a few lines of code into their existing website. From there the rest of the payment experience is hosted by Clearent.

  1. Add the payment button code to your webpage (see our examples)
  2. When a customer or client visits your website and clicks on the button a modal window pops up with a payment form that is hosted by Clearent

  3. Customer enters in their payment information
  4. Customer clicks the submit button
  5. Clearent processes the transaction
  6. Clearent sends the browser a response for the transaction
